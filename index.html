We Are Raising Disciples, Children with Values and Purpose At our orphanage, we believe every child was born with purpose. About Us

Who we are

We are a faith-driven community called to care for orphaned children and raise them into confident, responsible disciples of Christ.

Our Mission

Train orphans to lead, not just survive, but rise with boldness, identity, and purpose through the power of God's Word.

Our Vision

To build a generation of world changers,disciples who know their value, grow in faith, and become leaders in society.

Our Values

Faith in God, Healing and Restoration,Identity and Purpose,Responsibility and Growth
